Roy's Peak

Take in breathtaking views over Lake Wānaka, Mount Aspiring/Tititea and surrounding peaks after a steep climb through alpine meadows and tussock grasslands to the summit. This is a popular track and one of our best day hikes.

About

  16km return via same track

   Walking and Tramping
       5-6 hours return to Roy's Peak from Wānaka area in the Otago

  Seasonal Restrictions
       No fires are allowed.

In winter you will need to carry and be able to use alpine equipment such as avalanche equipment, ice axe, and crampons. The track may change between very slippery and muddy. No dogs allowed.
